How to buy?

1

Download the Phantom extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring SOL from an exchange or another wallet.

2

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like JUP, SushiSwap, or 1inch. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet". Then select Phantom

3

To swap ETH for $GETOR, select SOL in the "from" field and choose $GETOR in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4

Verify your transaction status in Phantom under the "Activity" tab or on Solscan using your SOL address. You import the token to your Phantom by pasting in the CA.
